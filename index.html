"Give me problems, give me work."

in the year 1878 i took my degree of Doctor of Medicine of the Unversity of Lundon, and proceeded to Netley to go through the coutse prescribed for surgeons in the army. having completed my studies there, i was duly attached to the Fifth Northumberland Fusiliers ad Assistant Surgeon. The regiment was stationed in India at the time, and before i could join it, the second Afghan war had broken out. On landing at Bomday, I learned that my corps had advanced through the passes, and was already deep in the enemy's country
